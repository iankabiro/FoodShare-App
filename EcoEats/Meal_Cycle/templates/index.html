About Us

EcoEats is a platform dedicated to helping reduce food waste and provide meals to those in need. Our mission is to connect people who have extra food with those in need, ensuring that no food goes to waste.

How It Works

Donate Food

Save the Environment

By reducing food waste, we help the environment and the community.
